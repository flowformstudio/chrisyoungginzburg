About Chris

A performer shaped by movement, theatre, and healing.

Known in the hip hop dance community as Fluidgirl, Chris has spent years developing a performance language rooted in street dance forms, theatrical storytelling, and embodied presence.

Her movement practice includes Popping, Waacking, House Dance, Footwork, Strutting, and Boogaloo, alongside training in Physical Theatre, Improv, Clown, and Mime. The result is work that feels both technically grounded and emotionally alive.

Chris is also a sound healer and holistic practitioner, and that dimension informs her artistry. Performance, for her, is not separate from healing; both are ways of listening, transforming, and helping people reconnect with themselves.

Performance Highlights

Selected areas of work

Medea Sirkas

Chris spent over six years as a core member of Medea Sirkas, one of the Bay Area's pioneering dance collectives, carrying forward Fillmore traditions of strutting and boogaloo.

Stage Performance

Her performance work brings together dance, acting, and character-driven movement for live audiences across theatrical and community-centered spaces.
